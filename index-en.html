Disabled Clu

The "Club de los disca" is the sector of the shelter dedicated to special animals, since they require a different type of care, paralyzed, blind or three-legged dogs.

Oldies Club

This section of the Campito Refugio is dedicated to introduce you to and tell you the stories of our adult and old camperitos. old dogs. The street is no place for any puppy, let alone an old one.

Campaign 2022
#NoChains

Dogs chained in a confined space for hours, days, months or even years suffer intense psychological damage. They are dogs that do not interact and when living in solitude they become depressed and are in a permanent state of anxiety.
Today we want to raise awareness and sensitize the many people who have dogs that live tied up all their lives and about the ordeal they make their pets suffer.
